WELCOME!

I'm Kelsey Drake.

As an aspiring UX Designer, I plan to spend each day learning more about how I can create products that will exceed users’ expectations and create a positive experience for anyone who chooses to use that product. I want to create experiences that benefit users and stakeholders alike, with priority on accessible design.

In my personal life, a lot of my plans revolve around my main goal of travelling the world. I want to meet and learn from people who come from all walks of life. I’d love to try new foods and listen to new music during my travels as well as learn how to do new things. For example, how cool would it be to dance on roller skates? One day, I’m going to do it. (In as many places as possible, of course).
