Bloomington Food Policy Council Redesign

Background

For this project, I was a team lead for a group of 4 team members. We were working on WordPress to redesign a local nonprofit's website: Bloomington Food Policy Council. During the semester, I helped them learn how to use WordPress, how to create sitemaps and wireframes, as well as conducting user tests. Together, we learned about web design and took what little information the website had previously to create our final design.

Our Wireframe

When we first created our wireframes, we all created what our ideal recreation of the website would look like, then presented those ideas, and finally came together to create a final wireframe. While some elements changed in the end due to the limitations in WordPress, we tried to keep as many of the elements as we could to stay true to the wireframe.

What We Learned

We learned a lot about how to use WordPress and our team supervisor helped us understand where we could improve our website in our different iterations. We learned a lot about typography, choosing colors, and maintaining accessibility standards.

Challenges We Faced

We faced many challenges, many of which were due to our lack of experience in WordPress. We struggled a lot with trying to have a sticky header, maintaining consistency in how far each of the elements expanded on the pages, and responsiveness. While some of these challenges we were not able to figure out in time, we did overcome many other challenges are are very proud of how the website turned out!
